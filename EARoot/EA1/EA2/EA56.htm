<p>Sensations that the patients themselves can feel that are<br/></p><p>evidence that they are experiencing a health related problem (symptom). Evidence recognized by the patient, doctor, or others of a health-related problem using hands, ears, eyes and nose (sign).<br/></p><p>No Distinction will be imposed between signs and symptoms. Ensures a common level of quality when identifying the appropriate entity.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p><font color="#ff8080"><i>Comment Stefanie: This is huge as it is defined now.
