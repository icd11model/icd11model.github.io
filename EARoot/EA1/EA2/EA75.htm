<p>A formal representation of a relationship that defines or characterizes the clinical attributes of an ICD11 category with respect to other categories and properties.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>This element groups definitional characteristics of a category if relevant for the coding.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p><font color="#ff0000">Stefanie's comments: One has to be very specific on what should be written in the textual definition and what should be specified in the section on definitional characteristics. Potentially there are two definitional parts that might be edited by two users and we do end up with a textual definition that does not match the definitional characteristics.</font><br/></p>
