<p>Each Clinical_Criteria is defined by a set of Clinical Characteristics (previously we called Definitional Characteristics). This means not all characteristics are necessary and only those that can be used for defining Clinical_Criteria will be captured. Clinical_Criteria has an attribute "algorithm" for rule execution and an attribute "technological level" intended to cover verbal autopsey, primary care, tertiary medicine, and related levels of technological capability. In addition, Plausibility_Rule such as AgeLimit and GenderLimit will be part of a Clinical_Criteria. <br/></p>
