Description by WHO


Diagnostic Findings: refer to results of any diagnostic procedure that are useful for identifying the diseases and their underlying physiological or biochemical cause(s).  Attributes such as diagnostic findings ensure a level of quality when identifying the appropriate ICD category. Typical knowledge from health sciences will be elaborated by the TAGs as defining characteristics of the ICD categories. In most instances, specialty adaptations and WHO Guidelines refer to this information.   The potential value sets include LOINC, SNOMED-CT or other laboratory information terminology.


Description by Extended Model

Analysis of the underlying physiological/biochemical cause(s) of a disease or condition
Any diagnostic procedure and its outcome that is necessary to identify the diseases
The group of diseases that are included in the category (ECG, NMR, Lab)
Ensures a common level of quality when identifying the appropriate entity
