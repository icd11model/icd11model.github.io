Synonyms:  refer to alternative names for the intended category indicating more or less the same underlying concept.  Common Terms and Medical Jargon may be included.
Synonyms are not necessarily meant to be used interchangeably with the Title. Their main purpose is to allow users to locate the proper code within the ICD.  The Title will have precedence over synonyms for international reporting. 

Synonyms will be taken from the ICD-10, International Nomenclature of Diseases or may be proposed by TAGs as a result of the web-based joint editing process.
