This is the manifestation of ICD 11 in publishable form. Note, this is the first time that a

Coded Element, in the historical tradition of the ICD codes, appears. It is a kind of linearization

entity, comprising a Coded Term. However, this Coded Term links back to an Entity ID, which

traces its lineage to the cloud layer and the ontology layer. Thus, stomach cancer as a Coded

Term would correspond to related concepts at the Category Level and the Ontology Level.

Nevertheless, a given linearization may assign different ICD codes in this current model. This is

highly controversial, and we will need to address this question. For example, in a primary care

linearization, stomach cancer might have been ICD code of I12.34. Whereas in a mortality

linearization, the code might be P45.56. Similarly in a morbidity classification the same concept

might be Q98.76. The implication is that the Coded Term is a subset of the Linearization rather

than that of the Category Layer. However the linkage to the Category Layer occurs through the

Entity ID. It is at the Linearization Layer that concepts of inclusions, index terms, exclusions

come to the fore. These again all derived from the structures and hierarchies associated with the

category layer.
